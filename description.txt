More Fruits adds 3 new types of Life Fruit that can be used in any combination after maxing Life Fruit! 

This is my second mod, I hope you enjoy it!


 - Life Cherry: Grants 5 extra max health per use. Crafted with 10 life fruit and 1 red dye at an Adamantite Forge.

 - Life Blueberries: Grants 5 extra max health per use. Crafted with 10 life fruit and 1 blue dye at an Adamantite Forge.

 - Life Grapes: Grants 5 extra max health per use. Crafted with 10 life fruit and 1 green dye at an Adamantite Forge.


 Depends on my first mod, Life Fruit Core, to change the colors of the health UI(7 colors added by combinations in this mod)
